How is Environmental Impact Calculated?
CO₂ Saved
Weight (kg) × CO₂ Factor
Example:
25 kg Paper × 0.82 = 20.5 kg CO₂
Factor depends on waste type & treatment
Water Saved
Weight (kg) × Water Factor (L/kg)
Example:
25 kg Paper × 26.5 = 662.5 Litres
1 kg paper recycled saves 26.5 litres water
Trees Saved
Weight (kg) × Trees Factor (trees/kg)
Example:
25 kg Paper × 0.024 = 0.6 Trees
1 kg paper recycled saves 0.024 trees (1 tree = 41.7 kg paper)
